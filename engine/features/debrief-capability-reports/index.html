Learning Engine

Debrief & Capability Reports

Turn every simulation into measurable organizational learning. Generate structured debriefs, identify readiness gaps, and provide leadership with actionable capability reports.

Debrief Components

Every completed simulation produces a comprehensive debrief that covers the full lifecycle of the incident response — from initial detection through resolution and retrospective.

Session Summary

Automated narrative summary of the simulation session covering incident type, duration, resolution path, and key decision points.

Timeline Replay

Step-by-step chronological replay of the entire simulation showing operator actions, evidence inspections, and state transitions.

Mistakes Analysis

Identify wrong paths taken, premature escalations, missed evidence, and incorrect hypotheses with explanations of why they were wrong.

Missed Evidence

Highlight evidence that was available but not inspected, and signals that were present but overlooked during investigation.

Escalation Review

Evaluate escalation timing, severity accuracy, stakeholder communication quality, and whether the right people were notified at the right time.

Team Readiness Gaps

Identify patterns across team members — common weak areas, role-specific skill gaps, and areas where the team consistently underperforms.

Recommendations

Auto-generated recommendations for follow-up drills, training focus areas, process improvements, and runbook updates based on simulation results.

Leadership Reporting

Executive-ready reports showing team readiness trends, simulation frequency, skill development progress, and organizational risk areas.
